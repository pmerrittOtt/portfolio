My Blog

Welcome to my corner of the internet — a place where web development, AI, music, humour, and everyday misunderstandings somehow all end up in the same conversation.

What started as a professional interest in technology gradually turned into something more personal: exploring how communication shapes everything we do. From building websites and working with Python and AI tools to navigating relationships, modern life increasingly seems to depend on clarity, context, and occasionally surviving a conversation you thought was going perfectly fine five minutes earlier.

These entries are a mix of observations, light-hearted reflections, technology insights, and the occasional comic-strip-worthy moment. Some posts explore AI and communication, others drift into music, creativity, culture, and the strange overlap between human relationships and machine interactions. Expect thoughtful ideas, dry humour, a few British music references, and the ongoing realization that both AI prompts and relationships tend to work best when ambiguity is kept to a minimum.

Or, as a certain Manchester band might put it — there is a light that never goes out… although sometimes it may end up sleeping on the sofa for the evening.
